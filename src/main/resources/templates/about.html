About

The lack of parking spots has taken its toll on society. All the waiting for a parking spot, all the hopeless driving around looking for a place to park, the stress and anger, etc.
Team MyParkingNav did indeed, perceive these kinds of situations and made an effort in creating this app, which in all hopes, will improve life od all people from different aspects.
